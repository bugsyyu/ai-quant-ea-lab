# ai-quant-ea-lab

Research workspace for evaluating and prototyping quantitative trading / Expert Advisor (EA) products.

用于评估与原型验证量化交易 / EA(Expert Advisor,智能交易系统)产品的研究工作区。

## Contents / 内容

- **[docs/market-research-ea-quant-ai-trading-2026.md](docs/market-research-ea-quant-ai-trading-2026.md)** — *Financial EA, Traditional Quant, and AI-Driven Quantitative Trading Systems: Market Research & Comparison Report* (English, July 2026). Covers the product landscape (forex/gold EAs, crypto bots, stock quant platforms, CTA systems), advantage/risk analysis of rule-based vs AI-driven vs manual trading, sourced public performance data with per-datapoint credibility labels, an MT4/MT5 EA ecosystem deep dive, product opportunity analysis, and MVP recommendations.
- **[docs/market-research-ea-quant-ai-trading-2026.zh-CN.md](docs/market-research-ea-quant-ai-trading-2026.zh-CN.md)** — 上述报告的**中文版**:《金融 EA、传统量化与 AI 驱动量化交易系统——市场调研与对比报告》(2026 年 7 月)。内容涵盖产品格局(外汇/黄金 EA、加密 bot、股票量化平台、CTA 系统)、规则型 vs AI 驱动 vs 人工交易的优势与风险分析、逐条标注来源与可信度的公开业绩数据、MT4/MT5 EA 生态深挖、产品机会分析与 MVP 建议。两版数据与结论一致,如有歧义以英文版为准。
